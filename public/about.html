About the project

Math and Physics Practice Lab is a worksheet-generator project designed to help students practice quantitative subjects faster. The current version focuses on Calculus I, Calculus II, Linear Algebra and Geometry, Statistics, Physics I, and Physics II.

The goal is to combine two things that are often separated: clean public-facing subject pages that can grow through search and content, and a practical generator app that lets students immediately build worksheets and export printable LaTeX PDFs.

What this version is optimized for

  • Testing the live app on GitHub and Render before a larger launch.
  • Creating separate subject entry points for better SEO and user clarity.
  • Preparing the site structure for future analytics, ad programs, and sponsorships.

What may be added later

  • Worked examples and theory summaries.
  • Topic-specific landing pages inside each subject.
  • Bilingual content and deeper exam-oriented collections.
